Katherine H. Ingram, Ph.D.

Associate Professor of Exercise Science,
Kennesaw State University, Atlanta, GA.

About

|

Director of NIH-funded biomedical research team focused on obesity, cardiometabolic health, and lifestyle factors (physical activity, diet, social factors), with a special interest in predictors of diabetes. Professor of exercise physiology, nutrition, fitness assessment protocols and design. Expert in wellness programming and weight-management counseling. Award-winning research mentor and supervisor. Partner in biosensor development program focusing on incorporating continuous glucose monitoring into wearable technology.
